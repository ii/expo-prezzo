What is Open Source?

Open Source Technology

Open source refers to creative work–often software–that is free to use, copy, and modify.

Open Source Creation

It is also a method of collaboration, where creative work is done in the open, and everyone is invited to contribute and share.

Free to use, remix, share, and evolve

With open source, you have more tools at your disposal, and you can personalize, improve, and share these tools as you please!

We think this is incredibly cool.

A career in free stuff?

When everyone can contribute, a project is able to improve in ways the original creators could only imagine.

A career in free stuff?

This tends to create exciting, resilient, popular, and adaptable work– work that businesses and other institutions want to fund.

Is it only software?

The term Open Source originated with software, but it is not limited to it. You can find examples of open source in art, design, construction, cuisine, goverment and law, music, and more.

Is open source right for you?

Probably! No matter your aptitude or interests, there is likely an open source project that you will love,and that would love to have you!
